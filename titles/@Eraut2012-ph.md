> Eraut, Michael. 2012. "Developing a broader approach to professional learning." [[Springer]]. http://dx.doi.org/10.1007/978-94-007-1724-4_2

> [!abstract]
> This chapter presents a broader approach to professional learning, developed from several research projects led by the author and his colleagues. The components of this approach include the following features: 1. The roles of informal learning, personal agency and teamwork in most workplace learning; 2. The decisions, processes and activities that encourage or ignore learning; 3. The roles of supervisors and managers in supporting learning through creating affordances for both personal and collective learning; 4. Recognising and understanding the emotional dimension of professional work; 5. Planning and supporting career development ; 6. Organisational learning and the management of change; 7. The influences of continuing professional education and human resource development.
