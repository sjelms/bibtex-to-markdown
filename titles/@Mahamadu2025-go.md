> [!abstract]
> Integration, modularisation and platforms have become ways of managing complex systems as well as the realisation of the principles of supply chain management and Lean thinking. Modularity addresses complexity and has become a popular paradigm for the development of products, services, processes as well as design of organisations and systems. For example, the construction industry is adopting modularisation as part of industrialisation to tackle age-old problems related to fragmentation. Some of the modular and industrialised construction developments include Modern Methods of Construction (MMC), offsite construction, robotics as well as Design for Manufacturing and Assembly (DfMA). Modularisation is also core idea of platform thinking which also shapes a significant part of the current digital economy. The module will prepare you with knowledge and equip you with the critical managerial abilities to manage and sustain change in industrial networks and ecosystems through integration of modularisation, supply chain management, lean and platform thinking. The module looks beyond projects towards managing products, processes and relations instead.
