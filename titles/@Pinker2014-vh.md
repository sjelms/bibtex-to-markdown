> In his 2014 article, "Why Academics Stink at Writing," Steven Pinker argues that academics produce poor writing due to the "curse of knowledge," professional narcissism, and a lack of incentives for clarity. He advocates for abandoning "academese"—marked by jargon, passive voice, and hedging—in favor of a conversational, "classic style" that focuses on presenting ideas clearly to a reader.
