> [!abstract]
> In exploring the construction of the machineries of occupational knowledge construction I have referenced general background characteristics of state welfare workers, but only intermittently have I woven points of discussion relating to these characteristics into analysis. I have done this primarily in order to facilitate interpretations of the survey data in Chapter 10 (allowing easier linkage to the qualitative analysis that precedes it). However, in this and the following chapter, I focus in greater detail on these and how they affect and are affected by emerging trajectories of learning in activity. Throughout much of this chapter, I specifically deal with the dynamics of an occupation in transition in terms of the experiences of veterans and newcomers.
