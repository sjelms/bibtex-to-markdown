> [!abstract]
> This white paper examines the concept of industrialized construction and its potential to transform the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) industry. It outlines current productivity challenges, explores the role of productization, design for manufacturing and assembly (DfMA), and prefabrication, and proposes a framework for shifting the construction value chain toward more integrated, technology-driven processes. The report highlights opportunities for improved efficiency, sustainability, and scalability while also discussing barriers to adoption and the role of digital technologies in enabling industrialized construction approaches.
