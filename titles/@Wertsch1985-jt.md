> During the last decade of his life Vygotsky’s account of the mediation of higher mental processes was constantly evolving. The sources of his ideas were varied, but at the most general level the writings of Marx and Engels played a central role. In their view labor activity is crucial in the creation of human consciousness. Indeed, they argued that we become human by engaging in the process of labor.
